Site Funding Note

Beagle Club uses affiliate links as its primary way of covering operating costs. This page explains what that means for you as a reader and how it relates to our editorial decisions.

Affiliate Links: What They Are

When a guide on this site mentions a product and links to it on Amazon, that link is typically an affiliate link. If you click that link and make a purchase within a qualifying window, Beagle Club receives a small commission from Amazon. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you had gone to Amazon directly — affiliate commissions are paid by Amazon, not added to your total.

What This Means for Our Recommendations

The Beagle Club Editors decide what to write about based on what beagle owners need to know. Whether a particular product has an affiliate link available plays no role in that decision. We mention products because they are relevant to the guide — not because we earn something when you click them.

If a product we mention stops being the right recommendation, we update the guide to reflect that. Affiliate income does not create loyalty to specific products.

Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). Beagle Club participates in the Amazon Associates program and may earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through links on this site.

No Sponsored Content

Beagle Club does not publish sponsored posts, paid reviews, or advertorial content. No payment is accepted in exchange for positive coverage of any product or service. Our editorial positions are not available for purchase.

Questions about this disclosure can be sent to hello@beagle-club.github.io.
