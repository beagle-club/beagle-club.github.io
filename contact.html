Get in Touch

Beagle Club is a community-minded site, and we genuinely welcome messages from readers. Whether you have a correction, a topic you'd like to see covered, or a follow-up to something you read here — this is the place to reach us.

Our editorial team reads all messages. Response times vary, but corrections and factual questions are treated as a priority. If a guide needs updating, we want to know.

What to Include

If you're writing about a specific article, including the page URL helps us route your message to the right person quickly. For general questions about beagle care, feel free to write as much or as little as you like — we'll do our best.

Email the Beagle Club Editors at: hello@beagle-club.github.io

We do not respond to unsolicited advertising or link exchange requests.
